Home

In order to use our services, you need to be logged in. Without signing in, you can still browse our gallery and filter through images. However, you cannot upload your own images or buy existing once, click here to create an account and be able to enjoy our awesome services.
If you already have an account, you can log in using the link shown above or by clicking here
Once you are logged in, you can see an upload zone in your home page. You can specify a description and tags for your image before you upload it. You can upload using drag and drop and manually using the upload button. On the same page, you can see your uploaded and bought images and delete them.

Gallery

In the gallery, you can browse the uploaded images. And filter them using the tags. You can type your search term manually, click on an existing tag or drag and drop a tag from the list to the filter. If you are logged in, you can select images you want to buy to be put in the cart either by clicking on the buy button or dragging the image you wish to buy to the cart icon.

Cart

In the cart, you can view the products you wish to buy. You can remove one product at a time or buy them all in one click.

Profile

On the right top side, you can see your profile picture and username. By clicking on the symbol, you will be taken to your profile. In there, you can view your personal data and edit it after entering your password.

Contact

If you wish to contact us, use the links to our social media websites we provide at the bottom.

About us

If you want to know about us, click on the Impressum link in the bottom of the website or click here

Admin

Admins have special powers. They cannot buy Products. They have the privilege to view all existing users and their uploaded and bought images. They can also remove users or their pictures individually. Admins have a special page accessable through the admin tools link near their profile pictures.
